Attic

Years ago I stumbled across someone’s personal website (that I sadly could not find again to credit) and they had a section of their site called a “Link Attic” and I loved the title. The idea is straight forward; a page on a site with random interesting links, unrelated subpages and other junk without a practical spot on a site similar to how you would store random junk in an attic.
